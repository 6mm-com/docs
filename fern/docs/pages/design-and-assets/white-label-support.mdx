White-label support helps partners integrate 6MM trading capabilities while keeping the product experience aligned with their own brand.

<h2 id="asset-versions">Asset versions</h2>

| Version | Description | Typical use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Branded assets | Include 6MM logo or recognizable 6MM brand elements. | When the partner wants to show that the trading capability is powered by 6MM. |
| White-label assets | Remove 6MM brand name, logo, and promotional language. | When the partner product should remain visually consistent with its own brand. |

<h2 id="supported-asset-types">Supported asset types</h2>

- Entry icons.
- Card-entry assets.
- Banner or feature-entry visuals.
- Splash or loading images.
- Empty-state and error-state visuals.

<h2 id="usage-boundaries">Usage boundaries</h2>

- White-label assets may only be used within the authorized partner product.
- Do not imply that 6MM has a direct end-user relationship unless separately agreed.
- Do not use white-label materials for unrelated products, advertising campaigns, or unauthorized third-party placements.
- Keep risk, compliance, and user-facing disclosures under the partner's own review process.

<h2 id="custom-needs">Custom needs</h2>

Contact the 6MM business or technical support team if you need custom dimensions, localized variants, or a different brand-display model.
