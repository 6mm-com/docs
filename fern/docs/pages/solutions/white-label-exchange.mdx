The White-label Exchange System is for teams that want to operate a branded digital asset trading platform without building every core exchange component independently.

<h2 id="included-capability-areas">Included capability areas</h2>

- Multi-terminal exchange experience
- Matching, account, order, position, and risk infrastructure
- Liquidity connection and market depth support
- Brand and visual customization
- Operational console and business workflow support
- Security architecture and compliance planning support

<h2 id="implementation-focus">Implementation focus</h2>

| Area | Documentation need |
| --- | --- |
| Product launch | Understand platform scope, user journey, and operating model. |
| Brand delivery | Prepare logo, entry banners, and white-label visual assets. |
| Trading operations | Understand order, margin, fee, and liquidation behavior. |
| Technical integration | Connect APIs, SDKs, webhooks, and monitoring workflows. |
