Brand customization should make the exchange feel native to the partner product while keeping trading information clear, consistent, and compliant.

<h2 id="customization-areas">Customization areas</h2>

| Area | Examples |
| --- | --- |
| Visual identity | Logo, favicon, accent color, entry banners, and campaign cards. |
| Entry placement | Homepage modules, asset pages, wallet pages, account center, and trading shortcuts. |
| User copy | Risk prompts, transfer copy, trading entry labels, and support messaging. |
| Asset packages | Downloadable partner assets for marketing, entry cards, and white-label materials. |

<h2 id="quality-checklist">Quality checklist</h2>

- Use the correct logo version for light and dark backgrounds.
- Keep trading actions visually clear and easy to distinguish from marketing content.
- Localize screenshots and downloads separately for Chinese and English pages.
- Keep support and risk prompts consistent with the actual trading workflow.
