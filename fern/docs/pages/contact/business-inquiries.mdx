<h2 id="6mm-business-conduct-policy">6MM Business Conduct Policy</h2>

6MM is committed to building a business environment based on integrity, professionalism, transparency, and compliance.

All employees, contractors, and related parties are required to comply with applicable laws, internal policies, and ethical business standards.

***

<h2 id="prohibited-conduct">Prohibited Conduct</h2>

Any improper conduct is strictly prohibited, including but not limited to:

* Bribery, kickbacks, or any form of corruption
* Conflicts of interest
* Market abuse or misuse of insider information
* Fraud or misrepresentation of any kind
* Violations of laws, regulations, or company policies

6MM maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward violations of these standards.

***

<h2 id="reporting-and-whistleblowing">Reporting & Whistleblowing</h2>

If you become aware of any improper conduct involving 6MM employees or related personnel, you are encouraged to report it through the official reporting channel:

<i class="fa-user-pilot-tie">:user-pilot-tie:</i> Reporting Email:

`report@6mm.com`

***

<h2 id="confidentiality-and-protection">Confidentiality & Protection</h2>

* Reports may be submitted anonymously
* All information will be handled with strict confidentiality
* Reports are used solely for compliance review and investigation

Confirmed violations will result in appropriate disciplinary actions and/or remedial measures in accordance with company policies and applicable laws.
