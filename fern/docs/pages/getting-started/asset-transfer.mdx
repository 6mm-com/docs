6MM operates under a partner-managed asset flow model.

6MM does not accept direct deposits or withdrawals from end users.

All user fund inflows and outflows are handled exclusively by authorized agents or partner platforms.

This design ensures clear separation of financial responsibility, regulatory scope, and user ownership between 6MM and its partners.

***

<h2 id="partner-managed-deposits-and-withdrawals">Partner-Managed Deposits and Withdrawals</h2>

* Users deposit and withdraw funds only through the partner platform
* 6MM does not provide on-chain or off-chain deposit addresses to end users
* 6MM does not directly custody or settle user funds outside of trading execution

Partners remain fully responsible for:

* User balance management
* Deposit and withdrawal processing
* Compliance and risk controls related to fund custody

***

<h2 id="automated-balance-transfer-to-6mm">Automated Balance Transfer to 6MM</h2>

To enable trading, users may transfer a portion of their partner-managed balance into 6MM via an automated internal transfer mechanism.

Key characteristics of this transfer process include:

* Transfers are initiated and authorized by the partner system
* The process is executed automatically at the infrastructure level
* No manual user interaction with 6MM is required
* Transfers occur in real time or near real time

From the user’s perspective, this process appears as a seamless balance allocation, rather than a traditional deposit.

***

<h2 id="account-structure-within-6mm">Account Structure Within 6MM</h2>

Within 6MM, transferred assets are logically allocated into two distinct internal accounts:

**Spot Account**

* Serves as the initial receiving account for transferred funds
* Acts as the asset pool for internal capital management
* Does not support direct trading activity

**Perpetual Account**

* Funds are transferred from the Spot Account to the Perpetual Account
* Only assets in the Perpetual Account can be used for order placement
* Margin, positions, and unrealized PnL are calculated at this level

This two-layer structure enables precise capital isolation, margin efficiency, and risk control for perpetual trading.

***

<h2 id="internal-transfers-between-accounts">Internal Transfers Between Accounts</h2>

* Funds transferred into the Spot Account can be freely and instantly allocated to the Perpetual Account
* Internal transfers are processed without blockchain interaction
* Transfers are designed to be low-latency and deterministic

This architecture ensures smooth capital movement while maintaining strict separation between asset custody and trading execution.

***

<h2 id="design-principles">Design Principles</h2>

The asset transfer system of 6MM is built around the following principles:

* No direct user deposits or withdrawals at 6MM
* All asset custody remains with partner platforms
* Automated, partner-authorized fund allocation
* Clear separation between Spot and Perpetual accounts
* Optimized for trading efficiency and risk isolation

Through this model, 6MM operates as a pure trading infrastructure layer, while partners retain full control over user funds and financial relationships.
