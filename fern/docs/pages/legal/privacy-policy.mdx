6MM respects privacy and is committed to protecting data processed within its trading infrastructure.

As an embedded perpetual trading infrastructure, 6MM does not directly onboard end users and does not collect or manage end-user personal information.

***

<h2 id="scope-of-this-policy">Scope of This Policy</h2>

This Privacy Policy applies to:

* Platforms, agents, and authorized partners integrating with 6MM
* Data processed at the infrastructure and system level

End-user privacy obligations remain the responsibility of partner platforms.

***

<h2 id="data-collected-by-6mm">Data Collected by 6MM</h2>

6MM may process limited technical and operational data, including:

* System and API access logs
* Order execution and trading activity data
* Risk management and margin calculation data
* Infrastructure monitoring and performance metrics

6MM does not collect:

* Personal identification information (PII) of end users
* User registration data
* KYC or identity verification information

***

<h2 id="purpose-of-data-processing">Purpose of Data Processing</h2>

Data processed by 6MM is used solely for:

* Order matching and trade execution
* Margin, liquidation, and risk management
* System stability, monitoring, and optimization
* Security, auditing, and incident response

Data is not used for marketing or profiling purposes.

***

<h2 id="data-storage-and-retention">Data Storage & Retention</h2>

* Data is stored in secure environments
* Retention periods are limited to operational and regulatory needs
* Access to data is restricted and logged

Data is deleted or anonymized when no longer required.

***

<h2 id="data-security-measures">Data Security Measures</h2>

6MM applies industry-standard security controls, including:

* Encryption of data in transit and at rest
* Access controls and permission management
* Continuous monitoring and security reviews

Security is integrated into system design.

***

<h2 id="data-sharing">Data Sharing</h2>

6MM does not sell or share data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Data may be shared only:

* With authorized partners for operational needs
* When required by applicable laws or regulations

***

<h2 id="partner-responsibilities">Partner Responsibilities</h2>

Partners are responsible for:

* End-user data collection and storage
* User consent and privacy disclosures
* Compliance with applicable data protection laws

6MM acts solely as a technical service provider.

***

<h2 id="policy-updates">Policy Updates</h2>

6MM may update this Privacy Policy as system architecture or regulatory requirements evolve.

Material changes will be communicated to partners through official channels.
