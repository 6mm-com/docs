6MM focuses on digital asset trading infrastructure for partners that need professional trading capability without building every core system from scratch. The platform combines matching, risk, liquidity, compliance support, API/SDK access, white-label delivery, and multi-device trading experiences.

<h2 id="positioning">Positioning</h2>

6MM helps partners launch and scale professional digital asset trading businesses through embedded trading infrastructure, institutional liquidity, white-label exchange systems, and compliance-oriented technical support.

<h2 id="core-capabilities">Core capabilities</h2>

| Capability | What it enables |
| --- | --- |
| Embedded API / SDK trading | Add professional perpetual trading to an existing app, wallet, broker portal, or business system. |
| Institutional liquidity | Connect trading products to deeper market depth and more stable execution conditions. |
| White-label exchange | Launch a branded exchange experience with matching, risk, operations, and multi-terminal support. |
| Compliance support | Prepare technical and operational materials for compliant regional launches. |

<h2 id="partner-ecosystem">Partner ecosystem</h2>

The 6MM ecosystem is designed for platforms, institutions, brokers, channel businesses, Web3 products, and teams that need reliable trading infrastructure without rebuilding every core component internally.
