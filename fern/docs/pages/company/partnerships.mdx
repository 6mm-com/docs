6MM partner onboarding is organized around choosing the right infrastructure path, validating the technical integration, and preparing the operational handoff before launch.

<h2 id="onboarding-stages">Onboarding stages</h2>

| Stage | Focus | Output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1. Business fit | Product model, target market, user flow, and launch timeline. | Recommended solution path and scope. |
| 2. Solution design | Embedded trading, liquidity, white-label exchange, or compliance support. | Integration architecture and responsibility split. |
| 3. Technical integration | SDK, API, webhook, authentication, environment, and monitoring setup. | Tested integration in the agreed environment. |
| 4. Launch preparation | Assets, risk prompts, support owners, incident path, and production checklist. | Production readiness review. |
| 5. Ongoing operations | Monitoring, issue escalation, product changes, and regional expansion. | Stable partner operating rhythm. |

<h2 id="information-to-prepare">Information to prepare</h2>

- Product type and expected user journey.
- Target regions, languages, and launch timeline.
- Preferred integration model: embedded SDK/API, white-label exchange, liquidity support, or compliance support.
- Existing backend, wallet, KYC, risk, and support systems.
- Production domain, callback paths, and technical owner contacts.
