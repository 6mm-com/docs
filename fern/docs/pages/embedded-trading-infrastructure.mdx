*Perpetual Trading, Seamlessly Integrated into Your Platform*

> 6MM is a professional-grade trading infrastructure designed for platforms, agents, and ecosystem partners.We deliver institutional-level perpetual trading capabilities through API, SDK, and embedded integrations—without operating a consumer-facing exchange.

***

<h2 id="trading-without-traditional-registration">Trading Without Traditional Registration</h2>

Traditional exchanges require users to complete registration, identity verification, asset deposits, and onboarding before trading—creating friction and limiting conversion.

6MM introduces an account-abstracted trading architecture that fundamentally redefines access to derivatives trading:

* Users do not register accounts with 6MM
* User identity, assets, and account relationships remain entirely within the partner’s system
* Assets are transferred and trades are executed directly inside the partner’s application

At the infrastructure layer, 6MM is solely responsible for:

* Order matching
* Margin calculation
* Liquidation logic
* Clearing and settlement
* Real-time risk management

6MM deliberately avoids any direct relationship with end users, positioning itself as a neutral, non-competitive trading infrastructure provider rather than a consumer-facing exchange.

***

<h2 id="clear-separation-of-responsibilities">Clear Separation of Responsibilities</h2>

6MM is built on a strict and transparent separation of roles, ensuring long-term alignment with partners and agents.

<h2 id="partners-manage">Partners Manage</h2>

* User acquisition and lifecycle
* Asset entry and exit
* Product positioning and user experience
* Application scenarios

  *(games, communities, fintech platforms, trading tools, etc.)*

<h2 id="6mm-powers">6MM Powers</h2>

* High-performance order matching
* Professional perpetual contract trading systems
* Real-time risk control and liquidation
* System stability, capital efficiency, and liquidity coordination

By design, 6MM never competes for end users and never monetizes user relationships.

All trading activity is initiated by partners, who retain full control over branding, users, and revenue models.

***

<h2 id="built-for-scale-and-institutional-performance">Built for Scale and Institutional Performance</h2>

From its core architecture, 6MM is engineered for high concurrency, ultra-low latency, and horizontal scalability, making it suitable for large-scale integrations and high-volume trading environments.

<h2 id="infrastructure-capabilities">Infrastructure Capabilities</h2>

* Stable order matching during extreme market volatility
* Native support for U-margined perpetual contracts
* Multi-level access control for platforms, agents, and sub-partners
* Extensible architecture for future asset classes and product types

For partners and agents, 6MM is not just a trading system—it is a scalable revenue engine capable of continuously converting user traffic into trading volume and fee income.

***

<h2 id="partner-first-by-design">Partner-First by Design</h2>

The core philosophy of 6MM is simple:

> Trading infrastructure should be as modular and accessible as cloud computing or payment systems.

We believe the next phase of derivatives market growth will be driven by distributed ecosystems, multiple access points, and diverse application scenarios—not by a single dominant application.

6MM is built to power this future—

**quietly, reliably, and at scale.**
